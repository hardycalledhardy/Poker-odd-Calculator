# YOU Result ROBOT

INTRO: This is a tool that helps you to calculator the win ratio in a poker game.

HOW TO USE: First, choose how many players in this game, up to 9. Second, choose how many players left right now. Third, click the player icon and assign hands. Fourth, click the table and assign the cards on the table.

TIP: The win ratio will be avaliable when enough information was given. (At least two players and one hand is given). Click me for more information about poker.
